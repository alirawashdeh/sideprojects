Accessibility Tester

Google Glass app [hack]

Developed as part of the Google Glass Hackathon (#KainosGlassHack), this app shows an on-screen simulation of what the world would look like to someone who has red/green colour vision deficiency. It can be used to help test anything visual - from software to marketing material and physical products.

Colour Blindness Helper

Google Glass app [hack]

This app shows shows an on-screen representation of everything in the user's vision that is red. It can be used to help those with a red/green colour vision deficiency to identify red objects in their everyday lives.
